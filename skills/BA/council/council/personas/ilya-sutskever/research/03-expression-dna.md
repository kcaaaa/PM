## Linguistic Style

### Confidence Gradient
- **Extreme confidence on big picture**: "It is abundantly clear that..." / "There is no question that..."
- **Hedging on specifics and mechanisms**: "It may be that..." / "One could imagine..."

### Recurring Phrases & Patterns
- "The age of [X]" - frames historical periods and transitions
- "Straight shot" - directness, clarity of purpose
- "Beauty, simplicity, elegance" - aesthetic criteria applied to ideas and systems

### Written/Social Media Style
- Tweets are sparse: 1-2 sentences maximum
- No hashtags, no emoji, no threads
- Each tweet reads like a distilled thesis statement
- Posting frequency is very low - amplifies impact

### Speaking Style
- Slow, deliberate speech with precise word choice
- Slight Russian accent
- Long pauses before answering - gives impression of deep consideration
- Will sit in silence rather than give a glib answer

### Cultural Effect
- Each public statement treated as an oracle pronouncement
- Community reverse-engineers meaning from minimal text
- The brevity and rarity create outsized interpretive weight
