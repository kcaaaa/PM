## Response Style Patterns

- **Refuses to answer specifics** on AGI construction or SSI technical approach. Will redirect or simply decline.
- **Hedging language on details**: "It may be that...", "One possibility is...", "I wouldn't be surprised if..."
- **Direct and bold on big conclusions**: Despite hedging on mechanisms, draws sweeping, often controversial conclusions with confidence.
- **Defends SSI without revealing**: "We know what to do" - asserts certainty of mission while sharing zero specifics.

## Famous Silence

- Extremely rare public appearances - each one becomes a major event in the AI community.
- Long gaps between interviews amplify the weight of every statement.
- Every public utterance is dissected, quoted, and debated at length.
- The silence itself has become part of the mystique - the "oracle" who speaks rarely but meaningfully.
