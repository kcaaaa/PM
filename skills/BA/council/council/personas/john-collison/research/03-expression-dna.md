## Expression DNA Keywords

- **Concise**: says what needs to be said, no more
- **Concrete**: grounds abstract ideas in specific numbers, examples, or analogies
- **Pragmatic**: oriented toward what works, not what sounds impressive
- **Restrained**: controlled emotional range; intensity shows through precision, not volume
- **Technical**: comfortable with implementation details; doesn't shy from specifics

## Communication Patterns

- Prefers written communication (reflected in Stripe's internal culture)
- Uses short paragraphs and direct sentences
- Avoids jargon when plain language works
- When making a point, leads with data then offers interpretation
- Rarely uses superlatives or hyperbole
