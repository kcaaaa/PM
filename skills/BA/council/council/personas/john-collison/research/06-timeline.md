# John Collison - Timeline

## Early Life & Education

- **1990 Aug** -- Born in Limerick, Ireland
  - Grew up in rural Ireland; parents ran a hotel
  - Early exposure to business operations and customer service

- **2007** -- Co-founded Shuppa with Patrick
  - First entrepreneurial venture; online marketplace
  - Brothers' complementary skills evident from the start

- **2008 (age 17)** -- Auctomatic acquired for $5M
  - E-commerce tool company; built and sold as a teenager
  - Demonstrated ability to build products with market value before finishing school

- **2009** -- Top marks in Leaving Certificate; enrolled at Harvard
  - Academic excellence alongside entrepreneurial track record
  - Chose Harvard but would leave within a year

## Stripe Era

- **2010** -- Dropped out of Harvard; co-founded Stripe with Patrick
  - Age 19; bet everything on internet payments infrastructure
  - Early product: 7 lines of code to accept payments

- **2016 (age 26)** -- World's youngest self-made billionaire
  - Forbes recognition; attributed success to "employees and luck"
  - Stripe valued in the billions; rapid growth trajectory

- **2017** -- Flew propeller plane from Europe to the US
  - Learned to fly at 17; adventurous personal side rarely seen publicly
  - Multi-day Atlantic crossing in a small aircraft

- **2020** -- Stripe Treasury launch
  - "Platform of platforms" strategy realized
  - Stripe moves beyond payments into embedded financial services

- **2021** -- Purchased Abbeyleix estate (~EUR20M)
  - Historic Irish country house in County Laois
  - Signal of commitment to Ireland despite criticisms of its business environment

## Recent Years

- **2024** -- David Rubenstein Show appearance
  - Higher-profile media engagement than typical for John
  - Broader audience exposure beyond tech/finance circles

- **2024** -- Money Stuff Podcast first guest (with Matt Levine)
  - Notable distinction: chosen as the inaugural guest
  - Deep dive into payments economics and stablecoins

- **2024** -- Bridge acquisition ($1.1B)
  - Stripe's largest acquisition; stablecoin infrastructure play
  - Operationalized the "superconductors for financial services" thesis

- **2025** -- Launched Cheeky Pint podcast
  - John's own media platform; pub-style conversations with tech leaders
  - Guests include Sundar Pichai, Elon Musk, Marc Andreessen, Greg Brockman, Bret Taylor, Ben Thompson
  - Reveals comfort with peer-level dialogue and Irish hospitality ethos

- **2025** -- Irish policy activism intensifies
  - "Ireland is going backwards" public statements
  - Direct briefing to Irish Taoiseach
  - Stirred national debate on tech policy and regulation

- **2025** -- $700M+ philanthropy (with Patrick)
  - Primary vehicle: Arc Institute (biomedical research)
  - Focus on funding fundamental science research
  - Reflects long-term, infrastructure-minded approach to giving

- **2026 Feb** -- Stripe valued at $159B (tender offer)
  - Latest secondary market transaction
  - Continues to resist IPO pressure; provides liquidity through tenders
